About Us



INTRODUCTION

RiverRose Distributors Ltd is a leading efficient importer and distributor of premium wines, whiskeys and beer. The company is ranked among the best in distribution of its products to all market segments As a company we pride in the quality and authenticity of products, excellent customer service, timely delivery and near to 90% customer order fulfilment, this is basically what has kept the business growing for the last seven (7) years. Our business has grown several times faster than any other known in the alcohol industry both in market coverage percentage and in outright figures. Our driving force is to ultimately have a one stop shop experience for our customers. Over the years we have partnered with manufacturers both local and international who provide a wide variety of products ranging from beer, wine, spirits and non-alcoholic drinks. We are a full service alcohol distribution company based in Kenya, with the aim of rendering quality services across the country. The primary approach of our company is based on the simple analogy that the success of our clients equates our success as well and this is our guiding principle in every transaction. Our company is composed of highly enthusiastic and experienced distribution agents who are ready to satisfy the market demands. As a company operated since 2015, we have always made real-time, customer centric decisions without the corporate bureaucracy and politics. We have developed and maintained lasting relationships with customers and vendors who share our commitment to offering quality products and services to the end consumer.

OUR BUSINESS

Riverrose Distributors Ltd has grown over the years and to conduct business we use structured methods to reach our clientele. We have a firm structured distribution network that involves a number of channels of distribution which include: -

Key Accounts:

These are on-trade Modern outlets. The accounts enables’ the company to move products in volumes. These accounts are key strategic partners to the business. They are customers that require special terms & touch hence the need to have direct contact persons for proper and clear communication. Basically the accounts contribute up-to 60% of the company’s universal customer base.

Delivery by order

This is a channel of distribution with a customer base of over 900 clients. It’s used to demarcate the target region into different market segments by assigning distribution sales reps. Additionally, the channel gives the company raw and real time data on the market dynamics. We would gladly say that it’s the largest volume mover for the company and it reaches out to greater masses. It helps in ensuring business sustainability through daily cash flow. Practictically we’d say these involves door to door delivery to customers which in return builds broader brand awareness and visibility while creating traction for the products. It also gives an opportunity to make the company’s Sales representatives to act as brand ambassadors for all products which helps to edifice and passing brand knowledge to the masses, just but to say we achieve unmatched brand building through route to market.

Regional market/shops.

The company has Regional presence in Nairobi County, Kiambu county, Kisumu county and Uasin Gishu County. This gives the company an opportunity to have nationwide distribution footprint hence giving the brands a countrywide reach translating to great numbers. Warehouse, Storage & Stock Control The company has an automated warehouse with a capacity of 9, 000sq ft. As a company we pride in having a state of the art inventory management system with top-notch stock replenishment module which guides on the strict operating reorder levels for different seasons of the year. For efficient and effective stock control, we regularly conduct the following: - Movement reports- which shows performance by brand, Cycle stock take- helps in monitoring the fast moving brands and overall stock take- which is done to give current stock holding position and help in valuation of the same. Additionally, the company enjoys automation of all its process and procedures starting from procurement, accounting, Point of sale, inventory management and the robust route to market channels.
